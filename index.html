After a cataclysmic explosion rips through Sol and nearby systems, the Galactic Empire is rapidly decaying. Corruption and piracy run rampant. But where there is chaos, there is also opportunity.

Seize that opportunity for yourself in this freeform space trading and mercenary game: buy newer and stronger ships, hire pilots to grow your fleet, or help a faction achieve its goals. Get credits to use toward your goal by delivering cargo, hunting bounties, ferrying passengers, or even turning to piracy. The choice is yours. Along the way, you will help decide the future of the galaxy.

About Naikari

Naikari is a 2-D freeform space trading and mercenary game, similar to games like Endless Sky, but with a heavier emphesis on survival and combat.

You pilot space ships from a top-down perspective which you can collect and customize more or less however you want. To get new ships, you can deliver cargo, hunt bounties, join and assist various factions, or even turn to piracy if you dare go against the Empire.

The game is currently a work-in-progress, and feedback is welcomed and appreciated. It is also open source, so if you want to try your hand in it, contributions are also welcomed and appreciated.

For frequently asked questions, see the Naikari FAQ. To discuss Naikari, leave feedback and suggestions, or ask questions, a Discussions page is available.

Home

A lightless void. That is what most of space is. Yet in spite of this, humankind has long looked up at the night sky in wonder, wishing to bask in the light of the tiny specks tucked throughout the sea of darkness.

After a cataclysmic explosion rips through Sol and nearby systems, the Galactic Empire is rapidly decaying. Corruption and piracy run rampant. But where there is chaos, there is also opportunity. Seize that opportunity for yourself in this freeform space-trading and mercenary game: buy newer and stronger ships ships, hire pilots to grow your fleet, or help a faction achieve its goals. Get credits to use toward your goal by delivering cargo, hunting bounties, ferrying passangers, or even turning to piracy. The choice is yours. Along the way, you will help decide the future of the galaxy.
